In-Depth Look

Each module is carefully designed to handle a critical aspect of gym management with ease and precision.

Member Management

Complete member lifecycle management from registration to renewal. Create detailed member profiles, assign membership plans, track subscription status, and easily search and filter your entire member database.

  • Member registration & profile creation
  • Membership plan assignment & tracking
  • Status management (active, expired, suspended)
  • Advanced search & filtering capabilities

Attendance Monitoring

Keep track of who's in the gym and when. Monitor daily check-ins and check-outs with timestamps, build attendance history, and generate reports to understand peak hours and member engagement patterns.

  • Real-time check-in / check-out logging
  • Attendance history per member
  • Peak hours & trend analysis
  • Automated attendance reports

Calories Burnt Tracker

Help members stay motivated by tracking their calorie expenditure during workouts. Log different exercise types, durations, and intensity levels to calculate and visualize progress over time.

  • Workout session logging
  • Calorie calculation per exercise type
  • Progress visualization with charts
  • Weekly & monthly calorie summaries

Billing & Invoicing

Streamline your gym's financial operations with automated billing. Generate invoices, process payments via M-Pesa integration, manage subscriptions, and maintain detailed financial records at a glance.

  • Automated invoice generation
  • M-Pesa payment integration
  • Subscription management & renewals
  • Complete financial record keeping

System Reports

Make data-driven decisions with comprehensive reporting. Get insights into membership growth trends, revenue analytics, attendance patterns, and fitness progress metrics — all from a single dashboard.

  • Membership growth reports
  • Revenue & financial analytics
  • Attendance trend reports
  • Exportable report formats

Role-Based Access Control

Security at every level. Each user gets a dedicated dashboard tailored to their role — Admins manage the entire system, Trainers handle member training, and Members access their personal fitness data.

  • Admin dashboard with full control
  • Trainer dashboard for member oversight
  • Member dashboard for personal tracking
  • Secure authentication & session management
